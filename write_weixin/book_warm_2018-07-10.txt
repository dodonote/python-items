<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; min-height: 1em;">

                        <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                            4 Prison 888
                        </p>
                        <p></p>
                    
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">4 888号监狱</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Commander Zadak was in his room at the biggest hotel in Kisangani．Suddenly，he heard the phone．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">在基桑加尼最大的饭店，扎达克司令正呆在他的房间里。突然，电话铃响了。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ‘Commander？’someone said，very quietly．‘A man and a woman—from Ship OM-45—in prison 888．’the phone went dead．Zadak put the phone down，stood up，and left his room．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“司令吗？”有人非常平静地说，“一个男的和一个女的——从OM-45号飞船上来的——正在888号监狱里。”电话挂断了。扎达克放下听筒，站起身，离开了他的房间。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Prison 888 was a big white building without windows，near Gog's house．There were a lot of guards at the gate，and some more guards marched past the bulding．All the guards carried guns．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">888号监狱座落在高格家附近，是座没有窗户的高大的白色建筑。监狱门口有许多卫兵，还有更多的卫兵从监狱门口走过。所有的卫兵都带着枪。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Commander Zadak walked across the road to the prison．‘I want to visit someone．My name is Commander Zadak．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">扎达克司令穿过马路来到监狱门前：“我是扎达克司令。我要见一个人。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ‘I'm sorry，Commander，’a guard said．‘We can't open the gate．Nobody can go in without a letter from Earth Commander．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“对不起，司令，”一个卫兵说，“我们不能开门。没有地球司令的信谁也不能进去。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ‘That's all right，’Zadak said，and walked along the road，past the prison．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“好吧，”扎达克说。他顺着马路从监狱旁边绕了过去。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    A guard ran behind him．‘Excuse me，Commander，’he said quickly．‘Please come back tonight．I can open the back gate at midnight．You can see the two people from Ship OM-45．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">一个卫兵从后面赶了上来。“对不起，司令，”他说得很快，“请您今天晚上来吧。午夜的时候我可以打开后门。您能见到从OM-45号宇宙飞船来的那两个人。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Zadak looked at the guard．‘Your captain's going to kill you．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">扎达克注视着那个卫兵：“你们队长会杀了你的。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    The guard smiled．‘My captain phoned you about the two people from OM-45．He's the brother of Captain Seru．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">那个卫兵笑了：“是我们队长给您打电话，告诉您OM-45号那两个人的事的。他是塞鲁船长的哥哥。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Zadak didn't smile．‘The prison commander is going to kill you and your captain．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">扎达克没有笑：“监狱长会把你和你们队长都杀掉的。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ‘It doesn't matter，’the guard said．‘We want to help．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“没关系，”那个卫兵说，“我们愿意帮忙。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ‘Midnight，’Zadak said，and walked away．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“午夜。”扎达克说了一声就走了。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    At midnight there were four guards at the front gate of prison 888，and there was one guard at the back gate．The guard opened the gate and Zadak went in．The guard said nothing．They walked downstairs into a big room．A lot of prisoners slept on the floor because there were no beds or chairs．Kiah and Rilla sat on the floor near the door．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">午夜时分，888号监狱正门前有四个卫兵，还有一个卫兵守在后门。那个卫兵打开后门，扎达克走了进去。卫兵什么也没说。他们走下楼梯进了一个大房间。那个房间里没有床也没有椅子，许多犯人睡在地板上。凯和瑞拉坐在门边的地板上。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ‘You can talk for five minutes，’the guard said quietly．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“你们可以谈五分钟，”那个卫兵悄悄说，“下一班卫兵十二点一刻来。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ‘The next guard arrives at 12．15．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">凯和瑞拉站起身：“晚上好，司令。”他们说。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Kiah and Rilla stood up．‘Good evening，Commander，’they said．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">扎达克看了看那些犯人：“这些人为什么被关在这儿？”他问凯和瑞拉。“高格不喜欢他们，所以把他们关了起来。我跟高格说了下雨的事，还有人工臭氧层的事，可是他根本不听。今天有人告诉我非洲那些幼林失火了，可是高格根本不想听火灾的事。”扎达克愤怒地冷笑了一声：“他也不想知道澳大利亚我那些树林的事。他从来不听我的。他总想着火星，从来不想想我们美丽的地球。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Zadak looked at the prisoners．‘Why are all these people here？’he said to Kiah and Rilla．‘They're in prison because Gog doesn't like them．I talk to Gog about rain，I talk to him about the AOL，but he never listens．Today，someone told me about some fires in the new forest here in Africa；but Gog doesn't want to hear about those fires．’Zadak laughed angrily．‘And he doesn't want to hear about my trees in Australia．He never listens to me．He's always thinking about Mars，Not about our beautiful planet Earth．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">凯认真听着。“别太在意，司令，”他平静地说，“请您和阿岱谈谈。他会帮忙的。他可以从月球回来帮助您。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Kiah listened carefully．‘Take it easy，Commander，’he said quietly．‘Please talk to Adai．Adai wants to help．He can come back from the Moon and help you．’
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“不行，”扎达克说，“今天下午我给他打了电话。他是个好人，也愿意帮忙。可我不需要帮助。我要阻止高格；我要杀了他。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
