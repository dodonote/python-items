<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal; min-height: 1em;">
    <br style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px;"/>
</p>

                        <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                            2 ROGER NOWELL
                        </p>
                        <p></p>
                    
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">2 罗杰·诺埃尔</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    John Law was ill because Alizon cursed him， and his son wanted Roger Nowell to question Alizon．Roger Nowell was a rich and important man in Lancashire， and he was the judge for all the villages near Pendle Hill．He lived at Read Hall， seven miles from Newchurch．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">由于艾丽森的诅咒，约翰·劳病倒了。他的儿子希望罗杰·诺埃尔审问艾丽森。罗杰·诺埃尔是兰开夏郡一个有钱有势的人，他是潘德尔山一带所有村落的法官。他住在里德宅院，离纽丘奇村7英里远。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    On the thirtieth day of March，Mr Nowell's men came to Malkin Tower．Mr Nowell wanted to see Alizon at once．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">3月30日那天，诺埃尔先生手下的人来到了马尔金塔，因为诺埃尔先生想马上见一见艾丽森。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    We walked from Malkin Tower to Read Hall： my sister Ali-zon，my brother James，and our mother，Elizabeth Device． I fol-lowed them because I didn' t want to stay at home with my grandmother． My grandmother was a difficult old woman， and I didn' t like her．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">我们一行离开了马尔金塔步行前往里德宅院：我的姐姐艾丽森，我的哥哥詹姆斯，还有我的母亲伊丽莎白·迪瓦斯。我之所以与他们同行是因为我不想和外祖母一起留在家里。我的外祖母是一个很难相处的老太婆，我不喜欢她。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Read Hall was an old house with a big garden and many old trees．Mr Nowell's servant opened the door for us．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">里德宅院是一所老房子，里面有一个大花园和许多古树。诺埃尔先生的用人为我们打开了房门。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Come in，'Mr Nowell said．He was a tall man with a lot of white hair．His black coat looked warm and expensive．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“进来，”诺埃尔先生说。他个子很高，白发苍苍。他的黑色大衣看起来既暖和又昂贵。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Alizon followed Mr Nowell into a room with a big fire．When I saw the fire， I wanted to go in，too！
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">艾丽森跟着诺埃尔先生走进了一个房间，那里面生着旺旺的炉火。当我看到炉火时，真希望自己也能跟着进去！</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Are you cold，little one？'Mr Nowell asked me．'Come in，and sit near the fire．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“你冷吧，小家伙？”诺埃尔先生问我。“进来，坐到炉火旁边去。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    I went across the room and sat down on the floor， next to the wonderful， hot fire．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">我穿过房间，坐到地板上，紧靠着那美妙的、暖烘烘的炉火。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Mr Nowell sat behind a big fable． Two or three men， in black coats， stood near the window． Alizon stood in front of Mr Nowell． Her long hair was dirty， and her old dress looked dirti-er．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">诺埃尔先生在一张大桌子后面坐下。有两三个穿着黑大衣的男人靠近窗口站着。艾丽森站在诺埃尔先生面前，她的长发脏兮兮的，身上的旧裙子比头发还要脏。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Two weeks ago，on the eighteenth day of March， you met John Law near Colne，'Roger Nowell said．His voice was slow and careful．'Tell me about it．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“两个星期前，也就是3月18日那天，你在科恩村附近遇到了约翰·劳，”罗杰·诺埃尔说。他的声音低沉而谨慎。“给我讲讲这件事。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'I asked for money，'Alizon said．'The pedlar was very an－gry and I didn' t like him． I was angry， too， and I wanted him to die！'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“我向他要钱，”艾丽森说。“小贩很生气，我不喜欢他。我也很生气，我希望他死！”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ' Tell me about your dog．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“给我讲讲你的狗是怎么回事。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    “The dog is my friend．'Alizon said slowly．'I wanted a friend， and I found that dog two months ago． I told my grand－mother， and she liked the dog， too．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“它是我的朋友，”艾丽森慢慢地说道。“我想要一个朋友，两个月前我找到了那条狗。我把这件事告诉了我的外祖母，她也喜欢那条狗。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Did the dog run after the pedlar？'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“那条狗是不是追赶小贩了？”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Yes， of course． I cursed the pedlar， and the dog ran after him！'Alizon said．' I'm sorry now， because Mr Law is ill．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“当然追了。我诅咒了小贩，那条狗便去追赶他！”艾丽森说。“现在劳先生病了，我很抱歉。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'She's a witch！'one of the men said quietly．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“她是个女巫！”一个男人轻声说。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Roger Nowell stood up and walked across the room to the door．'James Device， come in． We want to question you．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">罗杰·诺埃尔站了起来，穿过房间走到门口。“詹姆斯·迪瓦斯，进来。我们有话问你。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    James came in and stood next to Alizon．James was thirteen years old， nearly a man， but he was afraid of many things． He began to cry．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">詹姆斯走了进来，站在艾丽森身边。詹姆斯13岁，几乎是个男子汉了，但是，他对很多东西都感到恐惧。他哭了起来。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Don't be afraid，'Mr Nowell said．'we want you to talk abut your grandmother，Old Demdike．'
                </p>
                <p></p>

                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    The guards took my grandmother by the arms and carried her out of the door and put her on a horse．Everyone ran out of Ashlar House．They ran after the horses and shouted：'Kill the witch！'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">卫兵们抓住我外祖母的胳膊，把她带出门，放到一匹马上。大家全都跑出了艾什拉屋。他们追赶着马队，高喊：“杀死女巫！”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    I looked for Mr Nowell， but he was on his horse， too， and he followed the guards quickly through the village．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">我寻找着诺埃尔先生，可是他也上了马，跟在卫兵们后面迅速地穿过村子走了。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    Slowly， I followed my mother and James．Malkin Tower was my home， but I didn' t want to go back there． I was a little child， and I wanted someone kind to take care of me．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">我跟在妈妈和詹姆斯后面慢慢地走着。马尔金塔是我的家，但是我并不想回去。我还是个孩子，我希望有个慈爱的人来关心我。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);"></span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    We stayed at home days， because we were afraid to go out．James sat in front of the fire， with his dog， and talked．'Lan-caster Castle， Lancaster Castle，' he said， again and again． My mother hit me and shouted at me because she was angry with the rich men．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">因为不敢出门，我们在家里呆了好些天。詹姆斯和他的狗坐在火堆前，嘴里念叨着：“兰开斯特城堡，兰开斯特城堡。”他一遍又一遍地说着。我妈妈打我，冲我大嚷大叫，因为那些有钱人很让她恼火。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    But after three days，my mother suddenly said，'James！We' re hungry and we must eat！'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">3天后，妈妈突然说：“詹姆斯！我们饿了，咱们得吃东西呀！”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    James didn' t answer．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">詹姆斯没有答话。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    My mother went across the room to James and pulled his hair．'Get up！'she shouted．'Go out and find food for us！Your father isn' t here now； you must find food for us．' She hit him over the head．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">妈妈穿过房间走到詹姆斯跟前，揪着他的头发。“起来！”她大喊着。“出去给我们找点吃的！你爸爸现在不在了，你必须给我们找吃找喝。”她打了一下他的头。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    James stood up slowly．'Go out and find food，'he said．'I must go out and find food．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">詹姆斯慢慢腾腾地站起来。“出去找吃的，”他说。“我必须出去找吃的。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    It was dark， and James was out for hours． But in the morn-ing， he came back with a sheep．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">天黑了，詹姆斯已经出去好几个小时了。第二天早上，他带着一只羊回来了。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'I went to Barley，'James said happily．'I got this sheep，and now we can eat．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“我到巴利村去了。”詹姆斯高兴地说。“我找到了这只羊，现在我们可以吃饭了。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Get up， Jennet！'my mother shouted 'Come and help me！'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“起来，詹妮特！”我妈妈喊道。“来给我帮忙！”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    It was Friday， the tenth day of April． My family had some friends， poor people， and on that day they came to Malkin Tower．They came and asked about Old Demdike and Alizon，and they stayed to eat and drink．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">我们家有一些朋友，都是穷人。4月10日星期五那天，他们来到了马尔金塔。他们来询问老德姆代克和艾丽森的情况，然后留下来又吃又喝。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    I helped my mother． We cooked the sheep over a big fire，and our visitors ate with us． At the same time， they drank．They sat by the fire and drank，and talked about Lancaster Castle．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">我帮助妈妈干活。我们在一大堆火上烤羊，客人们和我们一起吃饭。他们还喝起酒来。他们围坐在火堆旁一边喝酒，一边谈论着兰开斯特城堡。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Let 's go there！'an old woman cried．'Let 's go to Lancast-er Castle and find Old Demdike and Alizon！'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“咱们到那儿去吧！”一个老太太大声说。“咱们去兰开斯特城堡救出老德姆代克和艾丽森！”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'We can curse the guards， and break down the door！' my mother said．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“我们可以咒死看守，然后把门打破！”我妈妈说。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Let' s bring them home！' said an old man．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“咱们把她俩带回家来！”一个老头儿说。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'Jennet，bring the bottle！ We need more drink！'shouted my mother．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“詹妮特，把酒瓶拿来！我们得再喝点！”妈妈喊道。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    I got up and took more drink to my mother．But I fell over one of the dogs， and the bottle broke on the floor． The drink was gone！
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">我站起来去给妈妈再拿些酒。可是我绊倒在一只狗身上，酒瓶掉在地上摔碎了，酒洒了！</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    ' You bad child！' my mother shouted．'You' re a witch， too，you know！'She got up and began to hit me．She hit me over the head and pulled my hair．An old man laughed，and then ev-eryone laughed．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“你这个坏孩子！”我妈妈嚷道。“你也是个女巫，你清楚！”她站起来打我。她打我的头，揪我的头发。一个老头哈哈大笑，大家也都跟着哄笑起来。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    I ran back across the room．I wasn 't a witch；I was a child，nine years old，and I hated my mother and all her friends！ My face felt very hot because I was angry． I left the room and went out of the house． It was afternoon， but the sky was dark with rain．Pendle Hill was dark，too．It sat quietly and watched me．
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">我穿过房间跑了回去。我不是女巫；我是个9岁的孩子。我恨妈妈和她所有的朋友！由于愤怒我的脸很烫。我离开房间，走出了这所房子。当时是下午，但是由于下着雨，天很黑。潘德尔山也是一片漆黑。它静静地坐落在那里，注视着我。</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                    'I'm going to Mr Nowell，' I said quietly， to Pendle Hill．'I'm going to tell him about my mother and her friends．'
                </p>
                <p></p>
            
                    <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">
                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);">“我要去找诺埃尔先生，”我平静地对潘德尔山说。“我要告诉他有关我妈妈和她的朋友们的事情。”</span>
                    </p>
                    <p></p>
                
                <p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; clear: both; font-family: &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: medium; white-space: normal;">

                        <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; color: rgb(178, 178, 178);"></span>
